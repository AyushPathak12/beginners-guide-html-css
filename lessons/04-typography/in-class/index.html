Font properties

  • font-family

    Specify a list of fallback font families.

  • font-size

    Size in px, em, %, and rems.

  • font-weight

    Weights, like normal, bold, lighter, 100, 400, 700,

  • font-variant

    Variants, like small-caps

  • font-style

    Styes, like italic, oblique

Text properties

  • line-height

    Not to sound rendundant, but it's the height of a line.

    You can squeeze the text close together.

    Or space it out, and improve readability. That isn't to say that you always want more space; it depends on the context.

  • text-align

    Do you want the text to align left, right, center or justified?

  • text-decoration

    Decorate your text, like none, underline, overline, line-through, blink (not all browsers support)

  • text-indent

    Put some horizontal space before the first line of text.

  • text-transform

    uppercase, lowercase, capitalize

  • letter-spacing

    Give your characters some breathing room.

  • word-spacing

    Add space in between words.

  • text-shadow

    Have fun, adding one or more shadows to your text, giving it some dimension.
