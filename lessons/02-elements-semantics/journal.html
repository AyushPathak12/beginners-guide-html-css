Journal

August 24th, 1952

Why Can’t I Catch this Bird?

I’ve struggled for so long to capture this one illusive species. It’s completely rare to find a road runner in the Utah desert, but here one exists. I want to capture it. I want to understand its origin, and its purpose.

Maybe in discovering its ways, will I begin to understand my own.

February 3rd, 1949

Construction, Acme, Apex

Warner University preaches itself as the ‘apex’ of education. Here I strive not just to satiate my understanding of birds, but to become one. I’ve found myself cross-dressing often in boa, plummage and more. What is it about their features that fascinates me so?

The more birds I capture, the more I study them, the more I find myself becoming one…

I’ve decided to pursue a bigger career in electrical drawings and schematics. All in my larger quest at understanding what it would take to become the ultimate of animals.

October 18th, 1947

Bird Flu

Like Darwin I’ve became fascinated with birds. Their plumage. Their ability for flight. I was always curious how they related to their bigger, extinct brethren, the dinosaur.

It’s fascinating to watch them flock. One of the only species to migrate, as an organic entity. I’m infatuated with them. And have chosen to purse my studies, and my career in understanding the the avian world.
